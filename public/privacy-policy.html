🙏 Joyful Prayer - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 30, 2026

1. Overview

Joyful Prayer ("App") is a prayer tracking and recording application developed for the intercession prayer team of a Christian church. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Prayer type (pastoral/church intercession), group name, personal name
  • Prayer Records: Daily prayer times, attendance records, Bible reading progress, memory verse records
  • Audio Data: Voice recordings for memory verse verification (stored locally on your device)
  • Notes: Spiritual guidance notes and prayer reflections

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version
  • Usage Data: Features used, session duration, crash logs
  • Local Storage: All data is stored in your device's browser local storage

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable prayer record tracking and statistics
  • To automatically submit prayer records to Google Forms
  • To verify memory verse recordings
  • To synchronize data across your devices (via Supabase, optional)
  • To improve app functionality and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All prayer records, personal information, and voice recordings are primarily stored in your device's browser local storage. This data remains on your device and is not automatically transmitted to our servers.

4.2 Cloud Synchronization

If you enable cloud backup, your data is synchronized with Supabase (our cloud partner). This is entirely optional. To disable synchronization, you can clear your device's local storage.

4.3 Google Forms Integration

When you submit prayer records, the app automatically pre-fills a Google Form with your data and opens it in your browser. You can review and modify the data before final submission. Google's Privacy Policy applies to this data.

4.4 Security Measures

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit (HTTPS)
  • No third-party analytics or tracking services
  • No advertisements or data selling
  • Regular security updates

5. Microphone Access

The app requests microphone permission to record memory verse recitations. You can deny this permission at any time through your device settings. Recordings are stored locally on your device only and are not transmitted without your explicit action.

6. Data Retention

  • Local Storage: Data remains on your device until you manually delete it
  • Cloud Backup: Data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time
  • Audio Recordings: Stored locally only; deleted when you clear app data

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data by:

  1. Opening the app settings
  2. Navigating to "Data Management" or "초기화" (Reset)
  3. Selecting "모든 데이터 삭제" (Delete All Data)
  4. This action cannot be undone

8. Third-Party Services

  • Google Forms: For prayer record submission (Google's Privacy Policy applies)
  • Supabase (Optional): For cloud data backup (Supabase's Privacy Policy applies)
  • No External Analytics: We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar tracking services

9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for adult members of the church intercession prayer team. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Parents who believe their child has provided information to us should contact us immediately.

10. Your Rights

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to request data portability
  • Right to disable cloud synchronization
  • Right to revoke microphone permissions

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you by email or through the app if there are significant changes. Your continued use of the app following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: parkks.joyful@gmail.com
  • In-App Support: Settings → Help & Support
